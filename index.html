Busuanga
Palawan

BUSUANGA, is the largest island in the Calamian Group of islands in the province of Palawan in the Philippines. Busuanga Island is the second largest island in the province after Palawan island itself. Also, Busuanga, officially the Municipality of Busuanga, is a 3rd class municipality in the province of Palawan, Philippines.
